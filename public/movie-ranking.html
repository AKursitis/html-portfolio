The Best TV Series According to Arnis

My Top3 Series

Mr. Robot

"Mr. Robot" is a psychological thriller television series created by Sam Esmail. The show follows Elliot Alderson, a brilliant but troubled cybersecurity expert with dissociative identity disorder. Elliot is recruited by an insurrectionary anarchist known as "Mr. Robot" to join a group of hacktivists called "fsociety." Together, they plan to take down corrupt corporations and disrupt the financial system. As the series unfolds, it delves into themes of identity, society, and the consequences of unchecked power. Rami Malek stars as Elliot Alderson, with Christian Slater portraying Mr. Robot. The series gained acclaim for its complex narrative, unconventional storytelling, and exploration of contemporary societal issues.

The Young Pope

"The Young Pope" is a drama television series created and directed by Paolo Sorrentino. The series revolves around Lenny Belardo, a newly elected and enigmatic American pope named Pius XIII, played by Jude Law. Pius XIII is a complex and conservative figure, known for his strong convictions and unconventional approach to papal duties. As the first American pope, he challenges the established norms of the Vatican and faces internal and external conflicts within the Catholic Church. "The Young Pope" explores themes of power, faith, and the intersection of religion and politics, while also featuring intricate character development and visually striking cinematography.

Ozark

"Ozark" is a crime drama television series created by Bill Dubuque and Mark Williams. The show follows the life of financial planner Marty Byrde, portrayed by Jason Bateman, who is forced to relocate his family to the Ozarks after a money-laundering scheme for a Mexican drug cartel goes awry. In an attempt to keep his family safe and repay his debt to the cartel, Marty becomes involved in the local criminal underworld. As the Byrde family navigates the dangerous world of organized crime, they face challenges, betrayals, and moral dilemmas. The series is known for its intense storytelling, complex characters, and the portrayal of the impact of crime on family dynamics. Laura Linney co-stars as Wendy Byrde, Marty's wife, and the series received acclaim for its suspenseful plot and strong performances.
